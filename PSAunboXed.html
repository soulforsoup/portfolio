13 July

We had some really useful insights this afternoon from #SmartPortChallenge2021 co-creators on #innovation opportunities around crew safety and health.

Some obvi...ous gaps in current solutions targeted at the mass market - they do not monitor/detect things like heat stress or fatigue; they are costly for large-scale implementation; they may not withstand harsh conditions or be deemed intrinsically safe for use in hazardous (explosive) areas; they rely on stable internet connectivity.

Thanks to our speakers from Association of Singapore Marine Industries, Hafnia, PSA MARINE, PSA unboXed, Workplace Safety and Health Institute (Singapore Ministry of Manpower) for sharing your experiences and challenges in addressing these issues. If you're a #startup with innovative solutions, come forward with them. Applications close on 10 August. Apply at https://pier71.typeform.com/to/kjjBPgLU

In case you missed the session, a recording is available at https://youtu.be/MUTQQwN0JRU
