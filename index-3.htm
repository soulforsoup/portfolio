Southeast Asia’s Leading Logistics Company for Road Freight & Cargo Delivery

With the largest truck fleet in Southeast Asia, Deliveree provides businesses with first, mid, and last mile transportation for road freight and cargo delivery.

Welcome to the revolution. Deliveree is unique to Southeast Asia for its blend of leading logistics services combined with the DNA of an advanced technology company. We are the market leading road cargo and freight transporter with the largest truck fleet across Southeast Asia. Our services include first, mid, and last mile transport for goods and merchandise inside cities as well as across country in Indonesia, Philippines, Vietnam, and Thailand. We blend our large scale, incredible prices, exceptional service, and third generation technology to deliver to our customers an exceptional experience at an exceptionally low price. Key advantages of Deliveree’s logistics service include flexible booking and low cost delivery. Flexible booking allows businesses and individuals to book with same day lead times as well as scheduling ahead up to 2 weeks. This creates a highly scalable road freight and cargo solution for businesses operating in fluctuating volume environments due to micro or seasonable business cycles. Low cost delivery is achieved by Deliveree’s unique clearinghouse approach to truck vendor inventory. By acting as a clearinghouse for excess capacity in the truck fleets of its tens of thousands of trucking vendors, Deliveree is able to bring customers heavily discounted prices. An additional advantage we bring to businesses and individuals across Southeast Asia include convenient mobile and web applications that can be used from any smart phone, laptop, PC, or tablet. Our technology makes it easy to book, manage, track, communicate, and complete road freight bookings whether it be a single truck or 100 trucks. Live track your drivers and live chat with them while en route. View photos and digital signatures as your goods are out for delivery. Manage your road cargo transport costs with a detailed digital wallet. Furthermore, take advantage of our new analytics dashboard which provides you with critical data, analysis, and key performance metrics of your historical logistics activities on Deliveree’s platform.
