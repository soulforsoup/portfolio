Katapult Ocean

We believe in a world where a thriving ocean is in harmony with economic development. That is why we invest in and support startups that have a positive impact on our ocean.

“We believe the best solutions come from global collaboration, so we’ve built a global ecosystem” – Take part in a global movement to save our oceans and find new opportunities.

  • Screening Read more

    Screening

    Our investment decision process includes several interviews where you will get to present yourself and your company and get to know our team. Interviews will contribute to our insight and assessment to team quality, impact potential, your product, business model, scalability & accelerator fit.

  • Due diligence Read more

    Due diligence

    Due diligence means understanding your organisation’s current situation and what is required in order to reach both business and technological milestones. The global potential of your company is also assessed.

    We additionally conduct a deep dive impact screening to assess the overall impact of your operations and how aligned and integrated it is with the company strategy.

  • Investment Read more

    Investment

    We invest in all the companies that take part in our accelerator program. The ticket size varies from USD 150 000 – 500 000, which we invest in your company in return for equity. We do charge a program fee which covers the cost of running the accelerator, but this can be deducted from our investment.

  • Accelerator Read more

    Accelerator

    In addition to investment into your company, you will take part in a comprehensive accelerator program tailored to ocean startups.

    During the program you will get hands-on mentorship from top global subject matter experts, serial entrepreneurs, business leaders and investors as well as access to pilot customers, test environments and real data.

    Learn more
  • Investor day Read more

    Investor day

    The program runs for 3 months and ends with an exclusive event for active and ocean relevant investors, where where you will get the opportunity to pitch to impact investors from all over the world.

  • Scale investment Read more

    Scale investment

    Katapult is raising a follow-on fund to increase our post-program capabilities and support our best performers. We plan to make investments in companies together with another lead investor, in Series A or similar sized rounds.

Invest, Catalyse and Steward

We invest in startups that build profitable businesses with a positive impact on our ocean.

We catalyse capital, talent, companies and startups to accelerate the blue shift in the ocean industries.

We steward ocean tech startups to achieve the UN Sustainable Development Goals with a main focus on SDG 14, Life Below Water.
